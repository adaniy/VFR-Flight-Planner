VFR FLIGHT PLANNER: tutorial

  1. First, select a region for the origin of your flight. The NOAA has a map here to show different regions.

  2. Next, input the ICAO codes for your origin and destination airports. Various ICAO lookup sites are available online.
  3. Select an appropriate cruising speed, altitude, and your estimated climb distance. If you don't know this figure, your Top of Climb (TOC) will not be accurately displayed, but your route will still be calculated.
  4. If you would like to perform weight and balance calculations, enter each weight and moment arm into the given input boxes. Should you need more, click the 'add input' button.
